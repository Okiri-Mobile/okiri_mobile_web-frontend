Refund Policy

1. Policy Overview

At OKIRIMobile, we value your trust and aim to provide transparency when it comes to refunds. Whether you’ve made a full payment, a partial one, or simply changed your mind, we treat each request fairly and promptly.

2. Key Definitions

  • Pre-Authorization (Preauth): A temporary hold placed on a customer’s payment method to verify available funds.
  • Capture: The actual charge made after a successful pre-authorization.
  • Refund: The return of funds to the customer after a capture.
  • Merchant: OkiriMobile
  • Customer: You, the user who initiates payment or subscription.

3. Refund eligibility

Scenario Full Refund Partial Refund Not Eligible
Service/Product not delivered ✓ ✗
Service/Product materially different ✓ ✗
Service/Product partially rendered ✓
Change of mind (within 24 hours) 50% After 24 hours
Subscription cancellations Prorated After billing cycle
Promotional or free trials ✗
Chargeback initiated by bank See Section 8

4. How to Request a Refund

  1. Submit Request:
    Email us at enquiries@okirimobile.com with subject line: Refund Request – [Transaction ID].
  2. Please Include:
    • Transaction/Order ID
    • Date of Purchase
    • Reason for Request (attachments optional)
  3. Acknowledgement: You’ll receive confirmation within 1 business day.

5. Assessment & Approval

Initial Review (1–2 business days): Support verifies eligibility.

Escalation (up to 2 more days): For complex cases, the Compliance Team will handle the review.

6. Refund Timeline

  • Initialization: Within 3 business days after approval.
  • Processing Times:
    • Card: 5–7 business days
    • Bank Transfers: 24-48 hours
    • Mobile Money: 2–5 business days
  • You’ll receive an email when the refund starts and once it’s complete.

7. Refund Deductions & Fees

  • Full refunds exclude non-refundable third-party gateway processing fees.
  • Partial refunds are based on actual service delivery.
  • All amounts are rounded to the nearest kobo/cent.

8. Dispute & Chargebacks Policy

  1. Please contact us First:
    Reach out to us before going to your bank—we may resolve things quicker.
    • If a chargeback is initiated, we’ll respond to your bank within 24 hours.
    • We’ll provide all necessary proof: logs, receipts, user actions.
  2. Chargeback Abuse Clause: Customers found abusing chargeback systems may face account restrictions or service suspension.
  3. Dispute Escalatin Tiers:
    4. Payment Partner Mediation (if unresolved)
    • Tier 1: Customer Support Response (24-48 hours)
    • Tier 2: Escalation to Compliance/Legal (48–72 hours)
    • Tier 3: Final review by Management (if necessary)

9. PreAuthorization Holds

If a preauth hold is not captured within 7 business days, it will automatically expire and release funds—no action required on your part.
